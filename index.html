Our Legacy

Founded in 1990, Luxtre has been a beacon of elegance and craftsmanship in the world of fine jewelry. Our commitment to quality and design has allowed us to create timeless pieces that resonate with our customers. With a focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing, we take pride in every creation, ensuring that each piece is not just an accessory, but a statement of individuality and sophistication. Join us as we continue to celebrate beauty and artistry for generations to come.
